About Me

Student at York University

Hello! I'm Daniel Chahine, a dedicated Computer Science student based in Toronto, Ontario.

Currently pursuing my Honours Bachelor of Science at York University, I'm passionate about technology and its transformative potential.

As a Tutor since 2020, I've provided personalized sessions in subjects like Computer Science, Calculus, Robotics, and more, actively promoting my services through online ads.

Beyond academics, my creativity shines through projects like "Mind Maze," an educational game, and various Python-based games like "Platformer," "Snake," "Pong," and "Flappy Bird."
